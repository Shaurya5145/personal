My Science Project

Soil types vs plant growth.

My Science Project

I studied how clay, sand, and loam soils affect seedling growth over three weeks. Key observations:

  • Clay retained water but root growth was shallow.
  • Sand drained too fast, plants wilted.
  • Loam had balanced moisture and nutrients; healthiest plants.

Book Review: 1984

George Orwell’s 1984 paints a bleak future where Big Brother watches all. Key themes:
